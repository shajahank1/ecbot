Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect and handle your personal information at ecBot.

Introduction

Welcome to https://ecBot.com. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and share information from visitors and registered customers of our multi-channel automation platform.

Personally Identifiable Information (PII)

PII refers to any information that can identify an individual, including but not limited to:

Name Address Phone Number Email IP Address Browser Information

PII does not include anonymous or demographic data that cannot be linked to a specific individual.

What Information We Collect

We collect basic user profile information from all visitors. For authorized customers, we gather additional details including name, address, phone number, email, IP address, and business information to provide our automation services effectively.

Collection by Third Parties

Third-party vendors such as credit card companies, banks, and Google Workspace APIs may collect information during your use of our services. We ask these vendors to disclose their use of your information, but we do not control how they handle it.

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third-party services to understand their data handling practices.

How We Use Your Information

We use your PII to:

Customize your experience on our platform, fulfill service requests, send emails about services and opportunities, respond to inquiries, and provide requested information and support for our automation solutions.

Information Sharing

We may share PII with other authorized customers for potential business transactions. We also share aggregated, anonymous data with third-party vendors for analytics and service improvement purposes.

All shared aggregated data is anonymized and cannot be traced back to individual users.

Storage of Information

Your information is securely stored using industry-standard security measures and is only accessible by authorized employees for the stated purposes. We implement appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data.

Your Choices

You may opt-out of receiving unsolicited information by contacting us directly using the contact information provided below. We respect your communication preferences and will honor your requests promptly.

Cookies & Tracking

Cookies help us understand your preferences and enhance security on our platform. They may also automatically log off authorized customers after a period of inactivity to protect your account.

We use login details like IP addresses and browser types for trend analysis and demographic tracking to improve our services.

Third-Party Partners

Some third-party vendors may access your PII on a need-to-know basis to assess eligibility for our services. This privacy policy does not cover their use of your data, and we encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

Legal Disclosure

We may disclose PII when required to comply with legal requirements, court orders, or to protect the safety and security of our users, employees, or the general public.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your PII from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. We are not liable for security breaches beyond our reasonable control.

Updating & Correcting Information

Visitors and customers can contact us at any time to correct, update, or review their personal information. We are committed to maintaining accurate and current data.

Deleting Information

You can request to delete or deactivate your PII by contacting us. Please note that some residual data may remain in our backup systems for a limited period due to technical and privacy requirements.

External Links

Our website may contain links to other sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies as they may differ from ours. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We recommend reviewing this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
